What is SaVest?

Helping you to achieve your financial goals without capital limitation

Our flagship product, SaVests is a Savings, Investment and Payment platform created with the aim of encouraging the age-long Wealth-Creation-Habit called SAVINGS among youths and all that desires to create wealth for themselves. With additional features for Bill Payments including Cable TV Subscription, Movie Tickets, Mobile Data and Airtime Purchase, Power and Utility Bills Payment and more

Our Mission

To raise a new generation of people empowered to achieve their goals without capital limitation

Our Slogan

Save to Create Wealth was born out of our understanding of power of Compound Interest and how it can help you to accumulate wealth by saving as little as N100 a day for the rest of your life.

Our Vision

To encourage Savings and Investments

We believe that to achieve Financial Goals requires some level of discipline. A lot of commitment together with self-conviction must be made to reach your Financial Goals. Just like any other goal in life. You need to move ‘one step at a time’, putting little amount of money away in a safe place at regular intervals until your financial goal is achieved. That is why we are offering you the SaVests platform built with the highest level of online security and managed by experienced banking professionals to help you fulfill your financial targets
