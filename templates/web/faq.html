Frequently Asked Questions

Activation is the process of unlocking your account to access the packages and services of the SaVests platform, this is required just once upon registering on the platform. The activation is also a means of verification.

Activation is set at NGN1,000 , and this is credited into your main wallet which can be withdrawn at any time.Â 

SaVests Direct allows you save a minimum of N1,000 periodically (Daily, Weekly or Monthly) towards a particular goal. With SaVests Direct you can watch your savings grow with the interest on your phone. You can also have multiple SaVests Direct savings account for multiple goals in mind (School fees, House rents, To buy land, To buy a car, Child naming and dedication ceremonies and more), With SaVests Direct you can make withdrawals at anytime from your savings and also lock your savings away into Savests Deposit for higher interest or to SaVests Wallet if you are the type that is not interested in earning interest.

SaVest Wallet is designed for some of our customers who want to keep money away for a period of time and may not be interested in earning interest for religious or ethical reasons. This product allow you keep your fund with SaVests and make withdrawals anytime without interest or penalty on withdrawals

Your card details are extremely secure and to confirm this, your card details are not stored on our platform, but it is kept safely by the payment gateway, and it is only called when you want to make payments

Once the maturity date of your savings and/or deposit arrives, your funds will be moved to your main wallet after which you can withdraw or re-invest the funds.

Typically, recurring payments are not mean to end until the maturity date of your savings, but if you choose to draw money from this account before the maturity date, It means breaking savings. And this attracts processing fee of 5% of the amount you want to withdraw from the savings.
But will have free withdrawal days which are the end of each quarter of the year:
28th March, 28th June, 28th September and 28th December
On these days, SaVests do not charge any fee from drawing from your savings
